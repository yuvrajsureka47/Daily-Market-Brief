GoI Removes Withholding Tax for FPIs; RBI Expands G-Sec Access
The Government of India abolished withholding tax and capital gains tax for Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPIs), and the Reserve Bank of India included long-dated government bonds under the Fully Accessible Route (FAR) for G-Secs.
