RBI Fires Forex Salvo: FCNR(B) Subsidy and Concessional Swap to Defend Rupee
In its June 5 policy meeting, the RBI kept the repo rate on hold at 5.25% but rolled out a powerful set of rupee defense measures. The central bank will fully subsidise the FX hedging costs for authorised banks raising fresh 3-5 year FCNR (B) deposits — a measure last deployed by Raghuram Rajan in September 2013, which drew $26 billion in flows. Additionally, the government removed capital gains and interest taxes on government securities for foreign investors, effective April 1, 2026.
