US Government to Test AI Models Before Public Release — Microsoft, xAI Signal Compliance
The US government is aggressively pushing for pre-release access to frontier AI models before public deployment. Enterprise AI Adoption
Anthropic's MCP Protocol Crosses 97 Million Installs — Becomes Default Agent Standard
Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP) crossed 97 million installs in March 2026. Every major AI provider now ships MCP-compatible tooling, and the protocol has effectively become the default mechanism by which AI agents connect to external tools and APIs. Why it matters: This is infrastructure-level lock-in, not just a product win. Similar to how TCP/IP became the internet backbone, MCP capturing the agent-connectivity layer gives Anthropic strategic leverage well beyond its own models. Enterprise AI adoption spending will route through MCP-compatible infrastructure, making this a capital flow indicator to watch.
